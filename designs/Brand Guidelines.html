01 — Logo & Mark

The FundWise mark

The logo is a diamond split vertically — left half in Settle Cyan, right half in Fund Purple. The split line represents the dual nature of the product: splitting expenses and pooling funds. It's legible at 16px and bold at any size.

08 — Voice & Tone

How FundWise speaks

Direct, confident, human. We're not a bank. We're not a crypto bro. We're the friend who actually handles the group tab — and does it cleanly. No jargon unless necessary. No fluff.

Settle actions
✓ Do
"Settle up · One click. Permanent."
✗ Don't
"Initiate an on-chain SPL token transfer to counterparty"
Balances
✓ Do
"Kiran owes you $30" · "You owe Asha $24.50"
✗ Don't
"Your net receivable position is positive $30.00 USD equivalent"

✓ Do
"Confirmed · recorded forever" · "Transaction confirmed in 1.2s"
✗ Don't

Errors

09 — Usage Rules

Do & don't

Protect the brand by following these rules consistently across every touchpoint.

✓ Do
— Use the logo on dark or white backgrounds only
— Keep the split line in the mark sharp and visible
— Use Settle Cyan for primary actions across the app
— Show green for positive, red for negative balances
— Write "FundWise" (capital F, capital W)
— Use Space Grotesk for all financial amounts
— Maintain minimum 16px clear space around the logo
✗ Don't
— Place the logo on gradient or patterned backgrounds
— Stretch, rotate, or recolor the mark
— Use purple for Split Mode or cyan for Fund Mode
— Use green/red for anything other than financial state
— Write "Fundwise", "fundwise", or "FUNDWISE"
— Add drop shadows or glow effects to the wordmark
— Use more than 2 accent colors in a single screen
