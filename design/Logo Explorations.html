Option A — Precision W · The Flagship

Thin, precise strokes on a deep forest-to-mint gradient. Glass highlight at the top of the container. The W has slightly asymmetric peaks — the left arm is the split (outward), the right arm is the fund (inward convergence). The dot is the settlement. This is the one.
