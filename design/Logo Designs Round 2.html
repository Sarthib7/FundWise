Round 2 · Concept-driven marks
Splitting, settling, flowing

Six new directions that lean harder into what FundWise actually does — split a bill, settle on-chain, route money between people. Same gradient, more meaning.

What each mark is "saying"
SplitOne amount divided among many. Bill-splitting at its core.
SettleMoney flowing from debtor to creditor. The on-chain transfer.
GroupPeople pooled together. The Group as the unit of trust.
Concept 06
Prism
SPLIT One ray entering, multiple rays leaving

A single beam of money enters from the top, splits into three even rays leaving the bottom — exactly what happens when one person pays a bill and FundWise divides it across the group. Direct visual metaphor; reads as a W from far away.

Concept 07
Converge
SETTLE Many rays in, one ray out — the inverse

The opposite of Prism: three rays converge into one. This is settlement — multiple debts netted into a single payment. Pair Prism (Split mode) and Converge (Fund mode) and you have a logo system that mirrors your two product modes.

Concept 09
Slice
SPLIT A bill divided, one slice highlighted

A circle cut into four — three quiet quarters and one bright slice popped out. The bright slice is "your share." Reads exactly like a bill split: the whole is the total, your slice is what you owe. Quietly clever, instantly readable.

Concept 10
Orbit
GROUP Members orbiting a shared tab

Four nodes evenly spaced around a central node — the group around the shared expense. The big middle dot is the bill, the four around are the members. Group-first, very approachable, very Splitwise-meets-Solana.

Concept 11
Knot
SETTLE Two arrows resolving the loop

Two interlocking arrows, one going down-left one going up-right — debt and repayment, the loop closing. The negative space in the center is a subtle "S" for Settle / Solana / Split. Most graphic and confident of the set; reads as an action mark.
