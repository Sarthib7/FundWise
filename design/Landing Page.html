Two modes, one app

Pick your flow

Whether you're splitting the restaurant bill or pooling funds for a group trip, FundWise has the right primitive.

⚡ Split Mode
Splitwise,
on Solana.

Log shared expenses, see who owes what, and settle up with a single on-chain transfer. Every settlement is permanent, auditable, and costs less than a cent.

  • Equal, percentage, or custom splits
  • Debt simplification graph
  • One-click SPL stablecoin settlement
  • On-chain tx signature as proof
🏦 Fund Mode
Reverse Splitwise —
pool first, spend after.

Everyone contributes upfront to a shared treasury. Spending requires a proposal and threshold approval — great for group trips, shared gifts, or recurring costs.

  • Squads multisig treasury
  • Proposal + threshold vote
  • Cross-chain contributions via LI.FI
  • Proportional refunds on close
How it works

Three steps to settled

Designed for people who want financial clarity without the friction.

01
Connect your wallet

Sign in with Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack. No email, no password — your wallet is your identity.

02
Create a group & log expenses

Invite friends by wallet address. Log who paid what. FundWise computes the fewest transfers to zero out all debts.

03
Settle with one click

Pay in USDC. Confirms on Solana in under 3 seconds. Permanently recorded on-chain — no disputes, no reversals.

Why FundWise

Built different

The familiar UX of Splitwise, the finality of blockchain.

⚡
Instant settlement

SPL transfers confirm in <3s on Solana. No waiting, no pending, no reversals.

🔒
Non-custodial

Your keys, your funds. FundWise never holds money — all transfers go wallet-to-wallet.

💵
Stablecoins only

USDC, USDT, PYUSD — any SPL stablecoin. No volatility. Always $1 = $1.

🌐
Cross-chain contributions

Fund Mode accepts contributions from any chain via LI.FI bridge.

📊
Debt simplification

Minimum transfers to settle a whole group — Splitwise-style, but on-chain.

🧾
On-chain proof

Every settlement stores a Solana tx signature. Dispute-free, forever.
