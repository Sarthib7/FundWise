01 — Logo & Mark

The FundWise mark

A clean W-form on a deep-to-fresh green gradient rounded square. The W reads as "Wise" and as two people's flows converging — the shared expense and the pooled fund. The dot beneath is the settlement: the moment it's resolved. Always use the provided SVG — never redraw it.

08 — Voice & Tone

How FundWise speaks

Calm, clear, human. We're not a bank — we're the friend who takes care of the tab and never makes it awkward. Speak like a person, not a product. Use plain language. Be direct. Never hide behind jargon.

Settlement actions
✓ Do
"Settle up · takes 2 seconds, costs less than a cent."
✗ Don't
"Initiate an on-chain SPL token transfer to your counterparty's wallet address."
Balances
✓ Do
"Kiran owes you $30" · "You owe Asha $24.50"
✗ Don't
"Your net receivable balance is +$30.00 USD equivalent."
Blockchain
✓ Do
"Confirmed · recorded forever." · "Done in 1.4 seconds."
✗ Don't
"Transaction validated by Solana consensus mechanism."
Errors
✓ Do
"Not enough USDC · top up your wallet and try again."
✗ Don't
"RPC Error 0x1A7: Insufficient token balance. Retry."
09 — Usage Rules

Do & don't

Protect brand consistency across every surface — app, landing page, social, and beyond.

✓ Do
–Use the logo on white, pale green, or very dark backgrounds only
–Write "FundWise" — capital F, capital W, no space
–Use DM Serif Display for all section and card headings
–Keep the gradient to buttons, bars, and the logo mark
–Show green for positive balances, red for negative — always
–Maintain generous whitespace — airy, not cramped
–Scale the mark proportionally — never stretch or distort
✗ Don't
–Apply the gradient to body text, captions, or large BGs
–Write "fundwise", "Fundwise", or "FUNDWISE"
–Add drop shadows or glows to the wordmark
–Use more than 2 accent colors in a single view
–Use Split Mode green for Fund Mode UI (use the blue accent)
–Place the mark on a gradient or photographic background
–Use Inter, Roboto, or system fonts — always load the brand fonts
