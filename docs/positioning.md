# FundLabs and FundWise Positioning

This adapts the FundLabs positioning strategy into repo-safe product language: consumer-facing copy leads with shared expenses, while unshipped Fund Mode, Fundy, and agent-commerce claims stay clearly marked as direction.

## Canonical Line

**Split expenses. Earn together.**

Use this as the FundWise hero tagline, submission headline, deck line, and top-level public positioning.

## Company Umbrella

FundLabs builds the financial layer for groups, human or AI.

FundWise is the first product in that strategy. It starts with shared expenses because that is the everyday group-money problem users already understand.

## FundWise Positioning

FundWise helps private Groups track shared spending, keep live Balances clear, and settle up with a verifiable Receipt.

The public story should feel like a better shared-expense app first. Wallets, stablecoins, and settlement rails stay in the background until a Member needs to review or sign a Settlement.

## Shipped vs Direction

**Split expenses** is the current product truth. Split Mode is the demoable MVP: Group, Expense, Balance, Settlement, Receipt.

**Earn together** is the product direction. It maps to Fund Mode: shared Treasuries, Contributions, Proposals, and later yield-bearing treasury strategy. Do not present yield or Proposal execution as shipped until the full flow is implemented.

## Product Family

**FundWise** is the consumer shared-finance app. Split Mode is live for the MVP; Fund Mode remains invite-only or planned until Treasury Proposal workflows are complete.

**Fundy** is the planned Telegram and personal-finance companion that runs the FundWise Agent. It starts read-only and draft-safe; money movement deep-links back to the web app.

**Receipt Endpoint / Payable Settlement Requests** are planned agent-facing infrastructure. They should expose exact live settlement intents and verified Receipts, not broad automatic payment authority.

## Messaging Hierarchy

Lead with: Groups, shared Expenses, live Balances, settle-up links, clear Receipts.

Second layer: Settlement finality, USDC, Solana, LI.FI routing, Zerion readiness, Scoped Agent Access.

Investor or roadmap layer: shared Treasuries, earning on idle Group money, Fundy, agentic commerce, machine-readable Receipts.

## Language Rules

Use `settle up` in broad consumer marketing.

Use `Settlement` in product, domain, API, and transaction contexts.

Use `Earn together` only with a clear current-state boundary: Fund Mode is direction until the Proposal and treasury-yield path ships.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim FundWise is the first crypto Splitwise.
- Do not claim automatic settlement, any-currency settlement, or any-chain settlement.
- Do not claim yield is live in the public app.
- Do not claim Fundy, Scoped Agent Access, or Payable Settlement Requests are shipped.
- Do not lead public copy with crypto terms unless the user is already in a wallet, Settlement, Receipt, or developer context.
