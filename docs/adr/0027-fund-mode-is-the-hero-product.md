# Fund Mode is the hero product

The north-star product is now Fund Mode: shared USDC Treasuries with Contributions, reimbursement Proposals, approvals, proof, and integrations; the next month prioritizes getting this into invite-only beta without claiming unshipped Proposal execution as live.
