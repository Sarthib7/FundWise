Problem

Group money lives in three broken places.

The chat

The Group decides what to buy, but chat does not become financial state.

The tracker

Splitwise-style tools record IOUs, then leave settlement somewhere else.

The wallet

Stablecoins can settle, but wallets do not understand Groups, Balances, or Receipts.

The problem is coordination plus settlement plus proof02 / 10
Speaker notes

What makes it good

User control

No auto-send

Trust
